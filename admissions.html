Admission Requirements

For Grade 11 Applicants

  • Grade 10 SF9 (Form 138) - ORIGINAL and 2 photocopies
  • PSA/NSO/LCR Birth Certificate - 2 Clear Photocopies (or Brgy. Certification ORIGINAL)
  • Certificate of Completion - 1 clear photocopy
  • Long brown envelope

For Grade 12 Applicants

  • Grade 11 SF9 (Form 138) - ORIGINAL and 2 photocopies

Application Procedure

Follow these steps to apply for admission to Nabua National High School Senior High School program:

1

Submit Application Form

Get and fill out the SHS Application Form from the school's Admissions Office or download from our website.

2

Prepare Requirements

Gather all required documents listed above. Make sure all documents are complete and properly authenticated.

3

Submit Documents

Submit all requirements and exam results to the Admissions Office for evaluation and processing.

4

Receive Admission Decision

Successful applicants will receive an admission letter with enrollment instructions and payment details.
