SHS Curriculum Tracks

Academic, TVL, and Arts & Design

Curriculum Tracks

Academic Track

  • ABM: Accountancy, Business, and Management. For those who plan to take up business-related courses.
  • GAS: General Academic Strand. Ideal for students who have not yet decided on a particular specialization.
  • HUMSS: Humanities and Social Sciences. Tailored for those interested in liberal arts, social sciences, and education.
  • STEM: Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. For students passionate about engineering, medicine, and computer science.

Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) Track

  • Home Economics (HE): Specializations in garments, tourism, health, and food and beverages.
  • Agri-Fishery Arts (AFA): Focus on plant and animal production, harvesting, and aquatic resources.
  • Industrial Arts (IA): Automotive, land transport, electronics, and construction.
  • Information and Communications Technology (ICT): System designing and customer service.

Arts and Design Track

Covers Theater, Music, Dance, Creative Writing, Visual Arts, and Media Arts.
